# Research: 파워플라자 (PowerPlaza) Company Footer Information

Date: 2026-04-24

## Summary

㈜파워프라자 (PowerPlaza Co., Ltd.) is a Korean power electronics company founded in March 1993, specializing in SMPS (Switching Mode Power Supply), DC-DC/AC-DC converters, and electric vehicle development. The company is headquartered in Gasan-dong, Geumcheon-gu, Seoul.
